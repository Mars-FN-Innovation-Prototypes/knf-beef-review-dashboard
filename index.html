Methodology and data qualityScope, dates, deduplication, coding, and limitations+
Scope

Only the eight products specified for this review. Chicken products, controls, and Frozen Beef Bolognese are excluded.

Written-review archive

153 unique records from Target, Amazon, Kroger, and Walmart. The default trend window contains 116 reviews dated Nov 1, 2024 through Jul 14, 2026.

Deduplication

Exact matches on source, product, date, rating, title, and review text were removed. The Amazon file declined from 600 raw rows to 60 unique rows across all products.

Topic coding

Transparent keyword rules tag texture, taste, sauce, value, packaging, and “product changed” language. Topic shares are directional.

Evidence layers

Dated written reviews drive trends. Retailer rating distributions include rating-only submissions and are retained as contextual snapshots.

Interpretation

Review language identifies where to investigate. It does not establish manufacturing, ingredient, supplier, or formulation causality.
